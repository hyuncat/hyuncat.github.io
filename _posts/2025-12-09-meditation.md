## A meditation

Dipping my toes into a cool pond\
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;encircled by firs,\
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;the murmurs of forest life,\
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;and an array of stars clattering about

the chill shakes my spine awake\
as I ease my body into its cobalt still.

I close my eyes.

Inside the water,\
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;thoughts rise and bubble,\
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;washing over myself.\
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Sometimes their suds turn\
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;into mermaid fins, and seashells.\
Outside the water,\
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;more thoughts\
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;racously bouncing\
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;tracing along the rim of the pond\
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;bristling against dim blades of grass.

Sighing, I lift myself up\
and sit atop the sill of the pond.\
The cold air breathes lukewarm onto my wet skin.

A cricket perches itself on my thigh\
an ugly thing, it ruins the mood.
